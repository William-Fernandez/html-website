Description

Good Time is the fourth release on MNTRV Records (MNT004), introducing a slightly more stripped-back and club-focused approach to the label's sound. A clean, groove-driven tech house track built for the dancefloor, driven by tight drums, a rolling bassline, and subtle vocal elements. Functional and direct, Good Time delivers solid dancefloor energy while remaining accessible, making it a reliable tool for both peak-time and late-night DJ sets.
